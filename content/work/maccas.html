McDonalds

Digital campaign design

I have worked on a lot of design for McDonalds. This particular campaign was to promote their special sauce, available over Australia day in certain stores.

The interactive map encouraged consumers to find their nearest participating store. I designed a responsive campaign site with multiple stages of release over the length of the campaign.
