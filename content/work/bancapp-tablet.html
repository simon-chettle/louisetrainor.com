Bancapp

UX /design for a tablet banking app

The project was to design and build a tablet version of the already existing mobile app. I was lead UX designer for this project. The aim was to provide some familiarity to the mobile app, while also making use of the extra space on the screen.

The homescreen included the same homescreen shortcut tiles in a carousel, but with the added benefit of having a much larger space for client campaigns.
