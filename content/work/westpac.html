Westpac

Digital UI design

The 'Home Owner's Hub', a minisite for Westpac, was designed to give new home owners information on their local amenities, a to-do list and helpful contacts.

Direct emails were sent to customers who had recently taken up a mortgage with Westpac. I was heavily involved in the UI design for all of these elements.
